FAQ

Do you provide warranties for your work?

Yes, we provide warranties for our workmanship and materials used. Our team will provide you with all the necessary warranty details upon project completion.

How do you ensure the quality of the construction?

We have a stringent quality control process in place. Our experienced supervisors conduct regular inspections to ensure that every aspect of the construction meets our high standards.

Can you help with obtaining the necessary permits and approvals?

Yes, we have a dedicated team that can assist you in obtaining all the necessary permits and approvals required for your project.

How involved can I be in the design process?

We encourage our clients to be actively involved in the design process. We value your input and collaborate closely to bring your vision to life.

How long does the home construction process typically take?

The duration of the construction process depends on the size and complexity of the project. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely. We offer a range of options to accommodate different budgets. Our team will work closely with you to find the best solutions that align with your financial goals.

Will I have a dedicated project manager or point of contact throughout the construction process?

Yes, you will have a dedicated project manager who will serve as your main point of contact. They will keep you updated, address your queries, and ensure a smooth construction journey.

What sets your company apart from other home builders in Bangalore?

We differentiate ourselves through our commitment to quality craftsmanship, personalized solutions, transparent communication, and a customer-centric approach. Our extensive experience and trusted reputation also contribute to our distinction.

What is your payment schedule and accepted payment methods?

Our payment schedule is structured to align with the different stages of the construction process. We provide clear and detailed payment terms in our contracts. As for payment methods, we accept various modes, including bank transfers and checks. Our team can guide you through the payment process.

What happens if there are design changes or additions during construction?

We understand that design changes or additions may arise during construction. We have a streamlined process in place to address these modifications, including assessing the feasibility, providing revised timelines and costs, and obtaining client approval before proceeding with any changes.
